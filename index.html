Projects

DojoIBL (2016 - Present)

DojoIBL is an open source platform that builds on a PaaS (Platform as a Service) cloud based architecture. It is deployed in Google App Engine (GAE) and it uses AngularJs to articulate the frontend.​ Its purpose is to provide tools and strategies to Teachers and Students to structure and organize collaborative Inquiry Based Learning processes.

LA4S (Learning Analytics for Small Universities) (2016-2017)

The main objectives of this European project 'Learning Analytics 4S' are to integrate already carried out experiences in the research topics LA and LPM, in combination with aspects of Learning Semantics (LS) and transfer innovations from these fields into the area of small size Higher Education institutions, like the University of Liechtenstein.​

weSPOT European Project (2013 - 2015)
